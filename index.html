Who Am I?

I’m Vaishali Kondoju, a Software Engineer with a specialization in Data Scientist with over 4 years of experience building scalable solutions across healthcare and tech domains. Currently pursuing my Master’s in Applied Data Science at Indiana University, I combine strong software engineering skills with a passion for analytics and real-world impact.

At Optum (UHG), I worked extensively with Oracle PL/SQL—developing stored procedures, packages, and performance-tuned queries for large-scale healthcare systems. I collaborated with cross-functional teams to deliver reliable, high-performance database solutions that improved data accuracy and system efficiency. Across both Optum and Tata Consultancy Services, I also built Python-based automation tools and web portals that streamlined workflows and enhanced internal operations. These experiences have shaped my approach to building robust, user-focused, and data-driven applications.
